What to Look for in a Power Bank

When choosing a power bank in 2026, consider these key factors:

Capacity: Measured in mAh. 10,000mAh is good for phones, 20,000mAh+ for laptops. Remember, actual output is about 60-70% of rated capacity due to conversion losses.

Output Power: Look for USB-C Power Delivery (PD) if you need laptop charging. 65W+ is ideal for most laptops. For phones, 20W+ is sufficient for fast charging.

Ports: Multiple ports let you charge more devices simultaneously. USB-C is essential for modern devices, while USB-A provides compatibility with older accessories.

Size and Weight: Higher capacity means more weight. Consider your use case: daily carry favors slimmer banks, while travel may accommodate heavier options.
