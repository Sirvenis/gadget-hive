Bluetooth Speaker Buying Guide

Sound Quality: Look for speakers with multiple drivers and passive bass radiators. Bose and JBL typically lead in audio quality.

Battery Life: 12+ hours is good for most uses. If you're often away from power, consider 20+ hour options.

Waterproof Rating: IPX7 means submersible in water. IP67 adds dust protection. Essential for outdoor use.

Portability: Consider size and weight. Smaller speakers are easier to carry but may sacrifice sound quality.

Extra Features: Power bank capability, speakerphone, multi-speaker pairing, and app control add convenience.
