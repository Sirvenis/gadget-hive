Wireless Charging Buying Guide

Charging Speed: Look for 15W if you want the fastest wireless charging. iPhones max out at 15W with MagSafe, while many Android phones support 15W Qi charging.

Form Factor: Pads are flat and compact, stands hold your phone upright for easy viewing, and multi-device chargers handle phone + watch + earbuds.

Compatibility: Most chargers work with any Qi-enabled device. MagSafe chargers are optimized for iPhone 12 and later.

Power Adapter: Many wireless chargers don't include a power adapter. You'll need a USB-C adapter with sufficient wattage (usually 20W+).
