Webcam Buying Guide for Streamers

Resolution: 4K is ideal for future-proofing, but 1080p is sufficient for most streaming platforms that compress video anyway.

Frame Rate: 60fps provides smoother motion, especially important for gaming streams. 30fps is fine for talking-head content.

Low Light Performance: Larger sensors and features like RightLight or adaptive light sensors make a huge difference in dim rooms.

Autofocus: Fast, accurate autofocus keeps you sharp even when moving. Some cameras offer manual focus for more control.

Software: Good companion software lets you adjust exposure, white balance, and other settings for a polished look.
