Home Office Desk Setup Tips

Ergonomics: Position your monitor at eye level, keep your keyboard at elbow height, and ensure your chair supports good posture. These adjustments prevent strain during long work sessions.

Lighting: Combine ambient room lighting with task lighting. Monitor light bars like the BenQ ScreenBar reduce eye strain without causing screen glare.

Cable Management: Use cable trays, clips, and sleeves to keep cables organized and out of sight. This not only looks better but makes cleaning easier.

Minimalism: Keep only essential items on your desk. Store supplies in drawers and use vertical space with shelves or monitor risers.
